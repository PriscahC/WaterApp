source: https://www.waterconservation.gov.hk/en/at-home/water-saving-tips/index.html

  • Shower for 5 - 10 minutes
  • Use water saving devices
  • Turn off tap while brushing
  • press the reduced flush button for liquid waste
  • Install flow controllers in water taps.
  • Don't defrost under running water
  • Don't wash utensils under running water
  • Reuse water to water plants
  • Wash cars with water bucket
  • Do not play with water
  • Fix leaking taps
  • Check and regulate water consumption
